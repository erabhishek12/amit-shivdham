Meet Our Leaders
Our Visionary

Our Founder

Anand Shankar Madhavan (1914-2007)

Anand Shankar Madhavan, a visionary and legendary luminary, dedicated his whole life for the cause of social service and promotion of ideal and quality education. A man of simple living but high thinking, he was a literary figure, man of academic repute, thinker and a writer par excellence. His innovated ideas, ideals and ideologies still guide and lead us on the path of our progress.
